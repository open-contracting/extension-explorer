Documentation

Implements fields and codes that are specific to European law.

For complete guidance on meeting the disclosure requirements of European law, see OCDS for European Union.

Guidance

If items have at most one delivery address, use the Location extension instead (see discussion).

Legal context

In the European Union, this extension's fields correspond to eForms BT-99 (Review Deadline Description), BT-163 (Concession Value Description), BT-109 (Framework Duration Justification), BT-505 (Organisation Internet Address), BT-508 (Buyer Profile URL) and BG-708 (Place of Performance). See OCDS for the European Union for the correspondences to Tenders Electronic Daily (TED).
